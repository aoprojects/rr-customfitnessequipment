Blue Carbon Bike

  • About Product
  • Product Specifications

    Can you call a bike Blue Carbon that isn't blue? We sought to answer that question. Based on the frame of a Schwinn AC Performance Indoor Cycle this bike not only fits the part, it looks it now too.

    If this shade isn't your favorite there is no need to be blue; we can adjust and customize the color tone to meet your specific design needs. This bike includes the magnetic resistance system, which means you can say kiss goodbye to indoor bike brake pad replacement for good. Combined with a super strength aluminum steel frame and coasting smart release features like a road bike this is the premium bike standard.

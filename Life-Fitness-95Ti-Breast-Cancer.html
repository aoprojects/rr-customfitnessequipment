  • "National" Breast Cancer Awareness Month (BCAM) is an annual international health campaign organized by major breast cancer charities every October to increase awareness of the disease and to raise funds for research into its cause, prevention and cure. The campaign also offers information and support to those affected by breast cancer.

    • Breast Cancer Awareness Month promotes the need to raise awareness for the disease and most importantly provide education for early detection and the need for mammographies. Established in 1985 by drug manufacturer Astra Zeneca, BCAM has now morphed in to a huge alliance of various groups and organizations that promote the cause under the pink ribbon originally conceived by the Susan G Komen Foundation in 1991 to show support for those that lost family or friends and for those still fighting to continue to do so. It wasn't until 1993, Evelyn Lauder, SC-VP of Estee Lauder Companies established the Breast Cancer Research Foundation and solidified the current two tone pink ribbon as the eternal symbol for the cause.

    TThe above history serves as a background on why the need for awareness is so paramount to the health and wellness of countless of thousands of women and even men everywhere. In keeping with giving back and promoting the cause for the entire months of September and October GymStore.com donate $50for each unit sold, to the Sloan Kettering Memorial Cancer Foundation; a leader in cancer research globally. If you want to support the cause and go for two-tone pink we will paint it complimentary.

    On the local level, many gyms have established a program where they offer matching donations based on the amount of miles run by their members on the Pink Ribbon treadmills donate a certain amount to the charity of their choice. We hope you will join us by helping to make the world a better place.

    For those of you that happen to love the color pink we can customize your order in various shades to match the design aesthetic you are going for without the Pink Ribbon branding and with whatever promotional or design decal you wish.

    We can also customize any other brand of treadmill you prefer in the same manner. Other popular favorites are the Life Fitness 95t Elevation Pink Treadmill, the Pink Cybex 530T Pink Treadmill, the Cybex 750T Pink Treadmill, and the Precor C956i Experience Series Pink Treadmill.

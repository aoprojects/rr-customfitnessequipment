Police Cruiser Treadmill / Black on White Treadmill

  • Product Details
  • Product Specifications

    This treadmill was created for one of northern New Jersey's busiest police departments, The City of Hoboken Police Department. Their need for a tough as nails commercial cardio workout spawned the idea for the creation. Whatever the organization or need for a sharp looking white on black treadmill this unit can be made to reflect your own personal style and be as creative as your imagination.

    Dimension

    32"W x 81"L (81cm x 206cm)
    Unit Weight 380 lbs
    Maximum User Weight

    400 lbs (181 kg)
    Power

    115V (+/-5%), 60Hz, 20A dedicated line; 208/220V (+/-5%), 60H, 15A dedicated line; 230V (+/-5%), 50Hz, 15A dedicated line
    Motor

    3.0HP DC Continuous duty with radial cooling fan and a High efficiency pulse width modulated (PWM) controller.
    Elevation Range

    -2 to 15%
    Speed

    0.5 to 12.4 MPH (0.8-20kph)
    Running Surface

    20"W x 60"L (50cm x 152cm)
    Programs

    Quick Start, Manual, 5K Run (10 levels), 9 Holes (10 levels), Heart Rate Control (60-95%), Calorie Goal (10 levels), Cardio (10 levels) and Weight Loss (10 levels)
    Console Features

    Upper Console: Dot matrix graphics of program, Large 1" LED display of distance, calories, METs, pace and heart rate, I/R port for set up.

    Lower Console: LED display of speed and dual function display of time and elevation.

    Safety

    E-stop to Emergency

    Warranty

    1 Year Parts & On-Site Labor

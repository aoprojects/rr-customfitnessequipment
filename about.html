About us

GS Customs

GS Customs is the sister company of Gym Store Inc; a NYC based dealer of new and remanufactured professional grade fitness equipment. While GS Customs has long specialized in restoring and customizing equipment for various customer needs / applications over the years; GS Customs was created to take that drive to the next level.

The GS Customs technicians were hand selected from some of the industry's best to offer not only top notch functional quality but also unparalleled customization and one of a kind branding ideas. We can work with your current equipment or supply you new/pre-owned. We can have your design be an eye catching show stopper to promote new business or a tough as nails workhorse that will look awesome and perform just as well

What we are doing?

With over 25 years of experience you can rest assured GS Customs is the ONE and ONLY worldwide professional resource for any custom gym equipment needs. Give us a call today and put us to the test with your ideas.
